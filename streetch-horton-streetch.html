Streetch Horton, Streetch

October 12, 2023 by greg

Ropes and fancy knots are the go to solution of the ages for sailors needing to tie down just about anything.  That’s all well and good, but sometimes we’re looking for something quick and easy and we don’t want to hunt down a 5ft piece of rope.

That’s why we keep a ton of bungees around.   We have dozens.  We hold down SUPs, we hold up curtains, we keep our scuba tanks from rattling.  This list goes on and on.   Even better, these bungees are plastic coated (no rust yet) and the length can adjust (and re-adjust) to suit your immediate need.  I highly recommend picking some up.
