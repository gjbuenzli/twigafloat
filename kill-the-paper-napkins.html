Kill the paper (napkins)

September 30, 2023 by greg

Cloth napkins are a no brainer, especially if you’ve already followed our advice and started using cloth paper towels.  If you really want, you can just use your cloth paper towels as napkins…or vice versa.  The principles still apply:  less waste, hard to source paper goods, less storage.
