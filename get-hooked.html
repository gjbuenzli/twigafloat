Get Hooked

October 16, 2023 by greg

One of the first things that anybody notices on Twig are the convenient hooks that we have everywhere.  “I’ve tried everything, and can’t get them to stick, and I don’t want to drill.  How do you do it?”.

We tried everything too.  We screwed in hooks.  We used command strips.  We tried a bunch of different type of hooks from Amazon.  Then we discovered the trick:  you need a robust sticky surface area to keep your hook hooked.  Then we tried these, and never went back.  I think we’ve bought about 4 packages of 20 so far, and have useful hooks everywhere on the boat.  Well, almost everywhere.  Nothing seems to stick to the vinyl ceilings of the salon and cabins.  I’m pretty sure somebody from Hogwarts cast a slippery spell on them.  Oh well, 85% of the boat ain’t bad.
