What SUP?

October 09, 2023 • By greg

Stand up paddle boards blasted on the scene about 15 years ago.  Now, it seems that every boat has at least one.  But this hack isn’t about that….

Here’s the secret:  have you noticed that your dinghy uses the same adapter as your SUP?  At least ours does.   That means that we can keep a single SUP pump in our dinghy that serves as our dinghy pump and our SUP pump.  No need to have an extra foot pump for your dinghy!  Oh, and if you have multiple SUPs (we have 3), you have backups (if you have the space to store them).

Ps. our favorite SUP is this one by Freein because you can add a sail to it and have a windsurfer.  Two toys in 1!  We like this SUP so much that we bought another one after I backed over the first one and shredded it.
