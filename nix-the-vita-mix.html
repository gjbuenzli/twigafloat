Nix the (Vita)Mix

October 02, 2023 by greg

Everybody has a VitaMix it seams.   They’re really expensive and big, so they must be good.  Plus, you can make just about anything with them.   We have one.   It came with our boat which was nice.   We never use it.  It’s big and a pain to clean.

However, we make our own hummus and have smoothies several times a week.  The kids make their own of both.  How do we do it?   The NutriBullet.  It’s small.  It’s easy to use.  The blender cup doubles as the serving cup.   Clean up is a snap.   You really owe it to yourself to get one.

Ok, I lied.  We used our VitaMix once.  The plastic gear of our NutriBullet broke.  It’s plastic, and it was about 10 years old.  It happens.   While we were waiting for a replacement gear to get delivered, we used the big blender.  Do yourself a favor and order some replacement gears as spares when you pick up your NutriBullet.
