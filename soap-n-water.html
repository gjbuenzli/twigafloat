Soap n Water

September 25, 2023 • By greg

Chances are that you either have a sink full of sudsy water in your galley, or you have to store dish soap by the gallon because you go through it so often.

Here’s a secret:  get a spray bottle and fill it up with mostly water.  Shoot a bit of dish soap in there and shake it up.   The exact amount is up to you.  Next time you do the dishes, spray that on the dishes instead of a full squirt of soap.  It’s mostly water, so you don’t have to run your sink.  It has a bit of soap, so you’re getting the dish clean.   Lather, rinse, repeat.

Pro tip:  this same method is used to create the sudsy foaming soap that you may use.  Instead of buying a refill of “foaming soap”, you can use (cheaper) regular hand soap and water (50/50) mix.
