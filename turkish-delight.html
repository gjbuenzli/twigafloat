Turkish Delight

October 11, 2023 by greg

You will get wet.  You’ll get wet early, you’ll get wet often.  You’ll get wet in the shower, and you’ll get wet while sleeping (rain).  Towels, however, are huge and bulky and hard to store.

Wrong!   I present to you the Turkish Towel.  These beauties are lightweight, dry quickly, and get the job done.  We have no problem storing enough for a family of five in a small cabinet.   There are some really expensive designer ones out there if that’s your jam, but we go with the inexpensive ones above.  You can get them at Costco too.
