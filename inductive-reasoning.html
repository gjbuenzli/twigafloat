Inductive Reasoning

September 29, 2023 • By greg

You have propane stoves, I get it.   You don’t need an electric stove.  Plus, electric stoves are dangerous and hot.

Sorry to say, but you’re wrong.  You need an induction cooktop, and here’s why

  1.  Sometimes you run out of propane, or the regulator breaks.  The latter happened to us, and it took weeks to source a replacement.  Our inductive stove saved us
  2. Induction stoves only work on special cookware.  That means that you’re not going to get a 300 degree singe when you put your hand on it.  Unless your hand is made of stainless steel.
  3. You probably have solar.  Or wind generation.  Or something like that to keep your electric topped off.  Do you have a propane generator?  Probably not.  The methane from your farts doesn’t count.   Inductive stoves are green
  4. You can put a much bigger pan on an induction stove.  This is our go to pan, and we can use it for everything because it has high sides.
  5. If the above doesn’t convince you, it’s nice to have another burner.
